Clay Impressions

Shapes, memories, moments
impressed in a material

To Press is an artistic project created by Sabrina Cardani and Elisabetta Schiavini.
Our aim is to convey art through the decorative objects we use in our daily lives.

Our Mission

We love to capture moments, thoughts and wishes and translate them into art by impressing images and words into clay.
We do this by utilizing traditional ceramic handcraft skills with a contemporary approach and a personal style.
We create personalized and unique art pieces.
To Press is environmentally conscious in its production: more clay, less plastic!
