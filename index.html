Clay Impressions

Shapes, memories, moments
impressed in a material

To Press is an artistic project created by Sabrina Cardani and Elisabetta Schiavini.
Its aim is to convey art through decorative objects we use in our daily lives.

Our Mission

We love to translate into art moments, thoughts, wishes, impressing images and words in the clay.
We use ceramic handcraft skills with a contemporary approach and a personal style.
We promote the conscious use of ceramic objects. More clay, less plastic!
