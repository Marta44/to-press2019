About Us

Sabrina & Elisabetta

We are ceramists and visionary artists. We work as a team designing and creating everything together in our own little ceramics laboratory in Jerago con Orago (VA), Italy. It is our dream that in the future we can expand our laboratory so that it can be used as creative space for ceramicists to meet, create and learn.
Ceramic is the medium that we use to express our artistic essence and that has guided us in our professional development.
Each piece of art we create for you is impressed with its own touch of individuality.
We use different mediums such as clay or grès porcelain and we have a variety of different decorative techniques at our disposal such as engraving, glazing or decalcomania.
This allows us to tailor any requests so that they are special and unique to you.
At To Press we love giving shape to our own and other people imagination through collaboration with clients, artists and companies who wish to create and convey art, fashion, culture and lifestyle with ceramic.

What we do

  • Limited editions
  • Furnishing objects
  • Unique pieces with personal and emotional meanings
  • Handcrafted clay and its decoration
  • Creation of plaster casts
  • Private ceramic courses
  • Ceramic firing on behalf of third parties
