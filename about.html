About Us

Sabry & Bess

"We are ceramists and visionary artists. We love giving shape to our and others imaginary. We design and create together impressing to every piece a touch of uniqueness.
We own a little ceramic laboratory in Jerago con Orago (VA), Italy. In the future we wish to destine an open laboratory as meeting place for ceramists and their artistic production.
Ceramic is the medium to express ourselves and that guided us in our professional development.
We use different media such as clay and grès porcelain and decorative techniques as decalcomania and engraving. In this way we can diversify the offer and meet different requests.
We love to collaborate with other artists, graphics, public or private companies that make use of ceramic to convey messages, ideas, projects and culture".

What we do

  • Limited editions
  • Furnishing objects
  • Unique pieces with personal and emotional meanings
  • Handcrafted clay and its decoration
  • Creation of plaster casts
  • Private ceramic courses
  • Ceramic firing on behalf of third parties
